About us

Assorty is a logistics firm that was founded in 2019 and it is one of the fastest growing logistic firms in South Korea. It distributes products from number of countries in different cities of the South Korea. On 1st of October 2021, the firm started online store with the purpose of providing cheap and quality products for Central Asians. As a person lives abroad he encounters many problems in living, job, environment and etc. He feels so lonely and outsider that even a trivial thing that reminds of his country make him happy abroad. Home sweet home but as a twist of fate people are living and working abroad. The Assorty online store has been designed to solve the problems of Central Asians(for the beginning) in terms of food and clothes since it is difficult to find halal and food of Central Asia in South Korea. It is true that there are some other companies that offer familiar services but what makes us unique is that you feel home with us you will not feel an outsider anymore, we give you the warmth of your home country by providing products and services as cheap as possible with the best quality.
